WHAT
IS CALLED ART?


Art, also called (to distinguish it from other art
forms) visual art, a visual object or experience
consciously created through an expression of
skill or imagination.

7 TYPES OF ART


The seven different art forms are Painting Sculpture,
Literature, Architecture, Theater, Film, and Music. However,
back in the day, the seven different art forms were called the
Liberal Arts, consisting of Grammar, Logic, Rhetoric, Arithmetic,
Geometry, Astronomy, and Music.
